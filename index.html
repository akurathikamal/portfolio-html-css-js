UL/UX

UI (User Interface) and UX (User Experience) design is integral to creating effective digital products. UI design focuses on the visual elements, such as layout, colors, and typography, ensuring that the interface is aesthetically pleasing and intuitive. UX design, on the other hand, emphasizes the overall user journey, aiming to make interactions smooth and enjoyable. It involves researching user needs, creating wireframes, and developing prototypes to test and refine the experience. Usability testing is crucial i n UX to gather feedback and make necessary improvements.
